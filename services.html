Our Premium Services

Comprehensive auto detailing packages designed to restore, enhance, and protect your vehicle.

Ceramic Coating

The ultimate protection for your vehicle's paint. Our professional-grade ceramic coatings create a hardened, sacrificial layer over your clear coat, providing extreme hydrophobic properties, deep gloss, and lasting protection against chemical washings, UV rays, and environmental fallout.

Starting at $799 / Includes Paint Prep
  • Multi-year protection (Up to 5 years guaranteed)
  • Intense hydrophobic properties formulation
  • Scratch and chemical resistance
  • Unmatched mirror-like gloss and depth
  • Easier maintenance washing

Interior Detailing & Restoration

A pristine driving environment is essential. Our interior detail goes far beyond a simple wipe down. We utilize steam cleaners, hot water extractors, and premium conditioners to sanitize and rejuvenate every inch of your cabin.

Starting at $199 / Varies by vehicle size
  • Thorough vacuuming of seats, carpets, and trunk
  • Hot water extraction for stain removal
  • Leather cleaning and pH-balanced conditioning
  • Plastics and vinyl deep cleaning and UV dressing
  • Interior glass polishing to streak-free perfection
Book Interior Detail

Paint Correction

Does your car's paint look dull or covered in spiderweb-like swirls in the sun? Our paint correction process safely removes a microscopic layer of clear coat to eliminate swirls, scratches, water spots, and oxidation, revealing the true color and clarity underneath.

Starting at $399 / 1-Step Correction
  • Full foam wash and chemical/mechanical decontamination
  • Multi-stage machine compounding and polishing
  • Removal of up to 90% of clear coat defects
  • Restores OEM clarity and reflectivity
  • Highly recommended prior to Ceramic Coating
Book Paint Correction

Headlight Restoration

Foggy, yellowed headlights not only ruin the appearance of your vehicle but severely diminish nighttime visibility. We permanently restore your lenses to crystal clear factory condition and seal them with ceramic coating to prevent future fading.

$99 / Per Pair
  • Multi-stage wet sanding to remove oxidized plastic
  • Machine compounding and polishing to optical clarity
  • Ceramic UV coating application for long-term protection
  • Improves nighttime visibility and safety instantly
